# charity: water – Water Drop Game 

In this game, users tap on mobile or click on desktop to burst blue water balloons. Each pop adds water to the bar and boosts your score. Watch out for dark balloons — those are polluted and will deduct points if popped. There is also a special golden balloon that gives extra points and an icy freeze balloon that freezes your game in harder game modes. Keep popping until the timer ends and try to fill the tank before time runs out.

This project was extremly challenging to make but also unbelievely rewarding being able to turn a sketch into a working game. I was able to push my skills in html, javascript, and css, allowing me to learn both the digital guts and design the interface of the game. 

The idea was simple at first but as I worked on it I was able to build two extra games modes with unique obstacles. This was really fun and I ran out of time to write so thank you for reading!

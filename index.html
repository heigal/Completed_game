Water Balloon Rush

How To Play

Tap on mobile or click on desktop to burst red water balloons. Each pop adds water to the bar and boosts your score. Watch out for dark balloons — those are polluted and will deduct points if popped. Keep popping until the timer ends and try to fill the tank before time runs out.
